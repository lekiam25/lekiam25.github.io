Hello! I'm Maikel,

I'm a 23 year old Gameplay Programmer, currently studying Creative Media & Game Technologies (CMGT) at Saxion Enschede. I have been interested in creating games ever since I found a link to a coding website in a magazine as a kid. Since then I have always been intrigued by how games work. I am constantly improving my skills and using new tools to create innovative game mechanics. That's why I chose this career path, there's always more to learn! I have been part of multiple group school projects, in multidisciplinary teams together with artists, designers and other engineers. Over the years I've picked up a lot of knowledge about the Unity Engine, which is my preferred engine.

Feel free to contact me through my links at the top of my page if you have any more questions! :D

Hell on Earth

A short game about destroying the earth as Greek Gods. You have to manage your followers and destroy the earth continent by continent. Every round, you get to choose between multiple miracle cards, these grant you new followers to increase your power.

Blind Faith

This game was made for a project with the theme "audio game". We decided to make an audio-only game aimed at blind people. It tells a story as you traverse a world with monsters and puzzles.

Spiritree

A simple tower defense game with a short narrative. You have to manage your resources and cut down trees to create defenses. Cut down too many trees and none will grow the next wave. My first project in Unity 3D.

Zoo Time!

A game where you can walk around in small interactive zoo. This game was made in a custom engine provided by my school.

OUTRUN

An arcade style autoscroller about a miner robot in space. Grab pick-ups and shoots rockets to avoid asteroids and run away from the giant space monster. Made in a custom engine provided by my school.

Personal Portfolio Term 1 2023

A prototype game made for a school subject. A small game with simple platformer mechanics similar to Hollow Knight. Click imagine to go to download page!
